# Bilnkit-Report

This report reflects the sales of an e-commerce company where the customer can only buy through the app, but the company has different supermarkets that function exclusively as warehouses.
Thus, through this report, we can obtain different information about the sales of different supermarkets, the average total sales, the total sales for different types of supermarkets, 
the total sales for different items, and their classification and visibility.

# Business Requirements

A - KPI's
  - Total Sales
  - Average Sales
  - No of Items
  - Average Rating

B - Chart's

  - Total Sales by Fat Content
  - Total Sales by Item Type
  - Fat Content by Outlet for Total Sales
  - Total Sales by Outlet Establishment
  - Percentage of sales by Outlet Size
  - Sales by Outlet Location
  - All Metrics by Outlet Type
